## Account and Subscription Questions

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="How do I create an account?" default-open="true">
    Visit nxtjob.com and click "Sign Up". Enter your email, create a password, and verify via the confirmation link. You start with a free trial to test core tools like resume builder.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="What subscription plans exist?">
    Choose from Basic (`$29/month`), Pro (`$49/month`), or Premium (`$99/month`). Premium includes unlimited consultations.

    | Plan     | Price      | Features                          |
    |----------|------------|-----------------------------------|
    | Basic    | `$29/mo`  | Resume builder, job tracker      |
    | Pro      | `$49/mo`  | + Cover letters, interview prep  |
    | Premium  | `$99/mo`  | + Unlimited 1-on-1 consultations |
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="How do I cancel my subscription?">
    Log in, go to Account > Billing, and select "Cancel Plan". You retain access until the billing cycle ends. Refunds apply within `14 days` for Premium.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="What if I forget my password?">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Reset Password" icon="key">
        Click "Forgot Password" on the login page.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enter Email">
        Provide your registered email address.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Follow Link">
        Check your inbox and click the reset link. Create a new secure password.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>

## Consultation Eligibility and Booking

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="Who qualifies for 1-on-1 consultations?">
    Pro and Premium users qualify. Mid/senior professionals (3+ years experience) get priority. Book via dashboard after `48 hours` of account activity.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="How do I book a session?">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Access Dashboard" icon="layout">
        Log in and navigate to "Consultations".
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select Expert">
        Choose from certified coaches by specialty (e.g., FAANG prep).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Pick Slot">
        View calendar and confirm. Sessions last `45 minutes` via Zoom.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>
